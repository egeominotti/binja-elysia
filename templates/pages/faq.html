To start a return:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to Order History
  3. Select the order and click "Return Items"
  4. Follow the instructions to print your return label

Once we receive your return, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. It may take an additional 5-10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement, depending on your bank.
