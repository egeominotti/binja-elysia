How to Return an Item

1

Start Your Return

Log into your account, go to Order History, and select the item(s) you'd like to return. Fill out the return reason.

2

Print Return Label

We'll email you a prepaid return label. Print it and attach it to your package securely.

3

Ship Your Package

Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location. Keep the receipt for tracking.

4

Receive Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days.

Return Policy

Eligible for Return

  • Items in original condition
  • Unworn and unwashed items
  • Items with original tags attached
  • Items in original packaging
  • Electronics in sealed packaging

Not Eligible

  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Personalized or custom items
  • Intimate apparel and swimwear
  • Items damaged by customer
  • Items without tags or packaging

Exchanges

Need a different size or color? Exchanges are easy!

Same Item, Different Size/Color

Select "Exchange" when starting your return. If the new item is in stock, we'll ship it as soon as we receive your return.

Different Item

For a different item, we recommend returning for a refund and placing a new order to ensure availability.

Refund Methods

Original Payment Method

Refunded in 5-10 business days after processing

Store Credit

Instant store credit available upon return approval

Return Shipping

Returns are free for the following reasons:

  • Wrong item received
  • Defective product
  • Item doesn't match description

For other returns, a 5.99 fee will be deducted from your refund.
